## Recurring site patterns

**Homepage:** emotional hero · mission sentence · Donate + Volunteer + Enroll · one parent quote · trust badges (501(c)(3), PATH) · impact stats

**Common pages:** About · Programs · Meet the Horses · Get Involved · Events · Contact

**Tone:** warm, hopeful, plain language; avoid clinical jargon

**Visual:** earth tones, barn/nature photography, generous whitespace
