# College Advising Venture — Product Plan

**Status:** Research consolidated 2026-06-12  
**Plan source:** Cursor plan `college_advising_venture_kb_a7f521c1`  
**Scope:** US undergrad (Common App + UC/CSU); San Diego-first; human advisor + AI/KB continuity

---

## Executive summary

The college consulting market (~$3B) sells **outcome anxiety** more than **provable product value**. Competitors bundle list-building, essays, timelines, and interview prep — but **effectiveness claims are mostly self-reported**, confounded by client wealth, and rarely independently audited (exception: Solomon Admissions).

**Product wedge:** Transparent SKUs + best-fit/net-price positioning + process scorecard proof + KB continuity between human sessions — San Diego-native (UC/CSU/CC transfer).

## Part 2 — How firms back up claims (evidence audit)

### Evidence types (trust ranking)

1. **Independent audit** — Solomon only (BPM accountant, Vault 50)
2. **Self-reported stats + honest disclaimer** — College Transitions, Admissionado FAQ
3. **Self-reported stats, no methodology** — Collegewise 98%, AcceptU 4x, Sierra tables
4. **Reviews (process, not outcomes)** — Collegewise Trustpilot; Rao Google 5-star
5. **Case studies / testimonials** — AcceptU videos; CMCA/Journey Ahead quotes
6. **Credentials + logo walls** — IECA, former AO (proves background, not causation)
7. **Aspirational copy** — Admit Advantage “certainty of success” (no stats)

### National claims vs. backing

| Firm | Claim | Backing | Proof weakness |
|------|-------|---------|----------------|
| Solomon | Per-school multipliers + applicant counts | **Independent accountant report** | Elite-client skew; no counterfactual |
| Admissionado | 97%; 5.3x advantage | Self-calculated; FAQ admits industry cherry-picking | No audit |
| Collegewise | 98% top-5; 1.5–4x multipliers | Internal L&D compile; Trustpilot | No outcome audit |
| College Transitions | 98% top-3; ~6x | List + **disclaimer** re talented clients | Self-reported |
| AcceptU | 4x selective; 90%+ top-3 | Admit counts + case studies | “4x” undefined |
| Sierra | 98% GPA 3.7+ to top 35 | Self-reported tables | No audit |
| College Zoom | 96% student top-3; 37 appeals reversed | Self-defined metric; attacks rival math | Not verified |
| Admit Advantage | “Certainty of success” | Logo wall only | **No statistics** |
| Rao | Named recent admits | Google reviews + blog | No percentages |

### SD locals

Testimonials and credentials dominate. Legacy publishes cumulative stats (550+ admits, $1.2M scholarships) — self-reported, no audit.

### Industry honesty gap

Almost nobody publishes: metric definitions, denominators, selection bias, counterfactuals, or **process metrics** (deadlines, aid literacy).

**Critique:** [Harvard Crimson 2025](https://www.thecrimson.com/article/2025/11/27/college-consultants-admissions-scrutiny/); [TIME on inequality](https://time.com/6237286/private-college-counselors-gatekeepers/).

## Part 3 — Weaknesses → product exploits

| Weakness | Our product response |
|----------|---------------------|
| Unverifiable 98% / 4x stats | **Process scorecard** + defined success (fit + net price understood) |
| Pricing opacity | **Published SKU menu** with hours, apps, deliverables |
| Prestige over fit | **List & Fit** with affordability layer |
| Ghostwriting | **Essay Coach** = rubric feedback only; authorship policy |
| Hourly snowball | Fixed modules + published overage |
| Aid afterthought | **Aid Letter Review** SKU |
| No between-session product | **KB continuity** (checklists, prep briefs) |
| SD virtual SEO shops | **San Diego-first** + local pathway KB |
| Transfer/CC/gap ignored | Non-traditional path SKUs |

## Part 4 — Our product SKUs (proposed)

| SKU | Price band | Exploits |
|-----|------------|----------|
| Clarity Call | Free 30 min | Sales opacity |
| List & Fit | $1,200–$1,500 (8 hr) | Financial fit gap |
| Application Season | $3,500–$4,500 | Hidden pricing; rush panic |
| Full Journey (9–12) | $5,000–$7,000 | Late-start premium |
| Essay Coach | $900–$1,200 | Ghostwriting ethics |
| Aid Letter Review | $300–$500 | Aid as afterthought |
| KB Continuity | Included / add-on | **Unique locally** |

### How we prove value

- Defined success metrics (not fake multipliers)
- Monthly process scorecard shared with families
- Reviews for experience (Google/Trustpilot)
- Anonymized private outcome log (with consent)
- Optional future aggregate audit if scale warrants
