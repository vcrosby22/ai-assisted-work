# Conversion and CTAs

**Purpose:** Design calls-to-action and flows that help users complete goals without friction.

**Harvest / review:** 2026-05

---

## Principles

| Principle | Practice |
|-----------|----------|
| **One primary action per view** | Especially mobile |
| **Verb-led labels** | “Donate now,” “Start application” — not “Click here” |
| **Reduce fields** | Every extra field drops completion |
| **Trust near conversion** | Security badges, policy links, testimonials at decision point |

---

## Patterns

- **Above the fold:** primary CTA + one sentence value prop.
- **Sticky header CTA** on long donate pages (careful with a11y focus order).
- **Secondary actions** visually weaker (outline vs solid).
- **Confirmation page** reinforces success + next step (share, receipt, volunteer).

---

## Anti-patterns

| Avoid | Why |
|-------|-----|
| Multiple equal primary buttons | Choice paralysis |
| Generic “Submit” | No affordance of outcome |
| Surprise fees at checkout | Baymard: top abandonment driver |
