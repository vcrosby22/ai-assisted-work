## Why this vertical is different

Fashion does not sell on features or copy — it sells on **image, identity, and desire**. That changes the design rules:

- The product photography (or its absence) is the single biggest conversion lever, not the layout.
- Restraint reads as expensive. Clutter reads as cheap. Luxury sites are mostly whitespace + one big image + restrained type.
- Demand is often **manufactured** (drops, scarcity, waitlists), not just captured. The marketing model is as much a design input as the brand.
